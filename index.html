About SAHEL COLLEGE OF HEALTH SCIENCES AND ADVANCED STUDIES GUSAU

SAHEL COLLEGE OF HEALTH SCIENCES AND ADVANCED STUDIES GUSAU is a premier institution dedicated to providing world-class education in health sciences. Established with the vision of transforming healthcare delivery in Nigeria and beyond, we are committed to nurturing competent, compassionate, and innovative healthcare professionals.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that our students receive the best possible education to meet the evolving challenges of the healthcare sector.

Our Mission

Sahel College of Health Sciences and Advanced Studies, Gusau is committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality education in health sciences and other professional programmes through innovative teaching, practical training, research, and community engagement. The college aims to develop skilled, disciplined, and socially responsible graduates who will serve with integrity, professionalism, and commitment to improving their communities and the nation at large.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution for excellence in education, innovation, and skills development, producing competent, ethical, and entrepreneurial graduates in health sciences and other academic disciplines who contribute meaningfully to societal development and national progress.

Core Values

    Sahel College of Health Sciences and Advanced Studies, Gusau is guided by the following core values:
  • Excellence – Commitment to high academic and professional standards in teaching, learning, and service.
  • Integrity – Upholding honesty, accountability, and ethical conduct in all institutional activities.
  • Professionalism – Promoting discipline, competence, and responsible conduct among staff and students.
  • Community Service – Dedication to improving the lives of people in Gusau, Zamfara State, and beyond.
  • Inclusiveness – Providing equal educational opportunities regardless of social or economic background.
  • Innovation – Encouraging creativity, modern learning approaches, and problem-solving skills.
  • Affordability and Accessibility – Ensuring quality education remains within reach of less privileged students.
  • Entrepreneurship and Skills Development – Preparing graduates for self-reliance, employment, and leadership.
  • Respect and Discipline – Fostering a culture of mutual respect, moral conduct, and institutional loyalty.

Our Departments

Department of Nutrition and Dietetics

Our Nutrition and Dietetics department is dedicated to training professionals who understand the critical role of nutrition in health promotion, disease prevention, and therapeutic management.

Students gain hands-on experience in our state-of-the-art nutrition laboratory and participate in community nutrition programs.

Department of Public Health

The Public Health department focuses on population health, disease prevention, health promotion, and health policy. Our graduates are equipped to address public health challenges at local, national, and global levels.

Students engage in field work, epidemiological studies, and community health interventions.

Department of Community Health

The Community Health department trains health workers who provide essential primary healthcare services at the grassroots level. Our program emphasizes practical skills and community engagement.

Students participate in community health outreach programs and gain experience in rural health settings.
