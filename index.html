About SAHEL COLLEGE OF HEALTH

SAHEL COLLEGE OF HEALTH GUSAU is a premier institution dedicated to providing world-class education in health sciences. Established with the vision of transforming healthcare delivery in Nigeria and beyond, we are committed to nurturing competent, compassionate, and innovative healthcare professionals.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that our students receive the best possible education to meet the evolving challenges of the healthcare sector.

Our Mission

To provide quality health education that produces competent healthcare professionals equipped with knowledge, skills, and ethical values to improve community health and contribute to national development.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in health education, recognized for excellence in training, research, and community service, producing graduates who are agents of positive change in healthcare delivery.

Core Values

  • Excellence in Education
  • Integrity and Ethics
  • Innovation and Research
  • Community Service
  • Student-Centered Learning

Nuhu Bashir Khalil is a seasoned administrator, entrepreneur, and public service professional with-disciplinary experience spanning politics, education, health services, and corporate management.
He received his early education at Dan Turai Model Primary School, Gusau (1998–2004), and completed his secondary education at Federal Government College, Sokoto (2004–2010). His academic and professional journey reflects a strong commitment to leadership, governance, and human development.
Mr. Khalil has extensive administrative experience in public service, educational institutions, and the health sector, where he has contributed to organizational development, policy implementation, and service delivery support. His involvement in the health sector includes management and administrative roles that support healthcare education, institutional coordination, and community-focused health initiatives aimed at improving access, efficiency, and service quality.
As the Chief Executive Officer of several private corporate organizations, Mr. Khalil has successfully led initiatives that promote education, healthcare development, youth empowerment, and employment creation. His work has positively impacted underserved and less privileged communities through capacity building, institutional support, and sustainable development programs.
Driven by a vision of inclusive growth and social responsibility, Nuhu Bashir Khalil remains committed to strengthening systems in education, health, and enterprise for the overall advancement of society.

Students engage in field work, epidemiological studies, and community health interventions.
